Intelligent Scholar Compliance Organizer I S C O An RFID-Based Compliance and Activity Tracker
for Iskolar ng Bataan with SMS Notification

Iskolar ng Bataan was meant to uplift scholars, yet it’s now a cycle of poor compliance tracking, delaying scholars, burdening officers, and forcing administrators to rely on outdated systems.

But we are IT students—when there’s a struggle, we innovate.

SCHOLARS

No more long lines, missing scorecards, or missed announcements. With a centralized feed, RFID logging, and SMS reminders, scholars stay on track easily.

OFFICERS

No more manual follow-ups or scattered updates. Officers can post activities, share announcements, and manage RFID logging within their municipality effortlessly.

ADMINS

No more confusion or manual tracking. Admins manage the program through post management, scholar data monitoring, and report generation.

WHY

I S C O?

The system introduces a smarter way to track compliance, transforming the outdated processes of Iskolar ng Bataan with powerful features such as:

1

Login Module

Enforces proper authentication between scholars, officers, and administrators. Ensures secure login and authorized access to relevant system functionalities.

3

Profile Management Module

Allows scholars, officers, and administrators to update personal information and reset passwords.

5

Scholar Management Module

Supports data migration into the system via CSV and enables tracking of scholar records, including personal and compliance data.

7

Notification Module and SMS Technology

Delivers timely system notifications for new posts and sends SMS alerts to scholars with insufficient compliance records.

9

RFID Logging Module

Establishes the process of recording compliance of each scholar through RFID scanning, with start-and-end controls for each activity.

2

Admin Management Module

Manages the creation and distribution of administrator accounts for secure system control.

4

Dashboard Module

Provides real-time summaries of registered, active, and inactive scholars, locality distribution, and scheduled activities.

6

Post Management Module

Provides administrators and officers with organized tools to create, filter, and archive posts, with officer-created posts requiring administrator approval.

8

Scholar Feed Module

Displays posts, along with essential information such as compliance status, ensuring scholars can easily access the latest updates and information.

10

Report Generation Module

Generates customizable reports from scholar records and activity logs, allowing administrators to securely download data.
